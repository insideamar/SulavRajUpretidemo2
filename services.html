Services

How I Can Help

Each service is tailored to your unique needs and goals.

Individual Therapy

Understand yourself more deeply

I offer a private, reflective space to explore your thoughts, emotions, and experiences. Together, we identify patterns, build insight, and support meaningful, lasting change.

Couple Sessions

Strengthen connection and communication

These sessions help partners understand recurring patterns, improve communication, and rebuild emotional closeness in a supportive and balanced environment.

Group Sessions

Grow through shared experiences

Group work offers a unique space to understand yourself in relation to others. Members learn through interaction, reflection, and supportive feedback.

MHPSS Consultation

Strengthening programs and teams

I provide consultation for organizations to enhance their mental health and psychosocial support programs through program design, capacity-building, and reflective guidance.

Supervision

Support for your clinical growth

Designed for mental health professionals, this focuses on case discussions, therapeutic process, and strengthening clinical thinking.

Reading Group

Learn, reflect, and think together

A guided space for those interested in exploring psychological ideas through selected readings and thoughtful discussion.

Starting Your Therapy Journey

Beginning thoughtfully, together.

We start with 4-6 evaluation sessions, meeting once or twice weekly. This helps both of us decide whether the therapeutic relationship is the right fit. If we continue, sessions will take place one to three times per week, with frequency adjusted based on your needs. Our work will focus on understanding your inner world and the deeper patterns that shape your feelings and relationships. Each session lasts 45 minutes.
