About Sulav Raj Upreti

Licensed Clinical Psychologist based in Kathmandu, Nepal.

Professional Bio

I am a licensed Clinical Psychologist based in Kathmandu with over 3.5 years of experience in assessment and psychotherapy. I hold an MPhil in Clinical Psychology from Tribhuvan University Teaching Hospital and am registered with the Nepal Health Professional Council. I also serve as an executive member of the Nepalese Association of Clinical Psychologists.

About My Work

A calm, confidential space to explore what feels difficult or repetitive.

In my work, I focus on helping you explore the deeper layers of your thoughts, emotions, and relational patterns. Together, we look at how past experiences shape present challenges and how these patterns show up in your daily life and relationships. I offer a calm, confidential space where you can speak openly, gain insight, and work through what feels difficult or repetitive. My aim is to support lasting change by strengthening your ability to understand yourself, relate more openly, and live with greater balance and meaning.

My Approach

Understanding patterns with depth and care.

My approach is informed by psychodynamic understanding, with attention to how relationships, early experiences, and repeated emotional patterns shape the present. Therapy is not only about reducing distress; it is also about becoming more familiar with the ways you protect yourself, connect with others, and make meaning of your life.

Together, we can explore how past experiences may be influencing current challenges, while also paying close attention to what happens in the here and now. This reflective process can help create more choice, openness, and balance in your relationships and daily life.
